1. Dashboard direction-aware color (BUD-02):
   - Go to /receitas, ensure the current month has a receita líquida. Go to /categorias if needed.
   - Open the dashboard, click "Definir metas". Set a TETO meta on a consumo category (e.g. Alimentação 30%) and an ALVO meta on Investimentos (e.g. 20%).
   - Log transactions in /extrato so Alimentação exceeds its teto and Investimentos is below its alvo.
   - On /dashboard confirm: the over-teto row is RED with "Estourou" + octagon-alert; a row at ≥80% but <100% teto shows amber "Aproximando" + triangle-alert; the alvo row below target is muted "Abaixo"; an alvo at ≥100% is GREEN "Atingido" + check. Confirm a gasto is NEVER painted red just for being a gasto.
   - Switch to the "Anual (YTD)" tab; confirm the same category order + color logic and the "Acumulado de 2026 (jan–jun)" window label. For a single populated month the Mensal and Anual % must read consistently.
   - Set a meta but pick a month with no receita; confirm the "Sem receita líquida em {período}…" copy appears, never NaN%.

2. "Qual reserva?" sub-flow (RSV-02):
   - On /reservas create at least one reserva.
   - In /extrato open "Novo lançamento", pick the "Reserva" category; confirm the "Qual reserva?" picker appears BELOW the categoria select (same dialog, progressive disclosure) with the helper "Este lançamento será registrado como aporte nesta reserva." Submit and confirm a linked entrada appears in that reserva's ledger (/reservas/[id]).
   - Inline-re-tag an existing transaction to "Reserva" in the Extrato; confirm a focused picker dialog opens and the aporte links.
   - Confirm on /dashboard the aporte raised the Investimentos/alocação line and did NOT raise any consumo line.

3. Reserva progress bar only with alvo (RSV-01/05):
   - Create one reserva WITH an alvo and one WITHOUT.
   - Confirm the progress bar appears only on the one with an alvo (the no-alvo card shows no bar at all, not a 0% bar).
   - Register a saída larger than the saldo; confirm it is blocked with the "A saída não pode ser maior que o saldo da reserva ({R$})." FieldError. Then a valid saída; confirm the derived saldo drops.
