<objective>
Build the Santander PDF text extractor as a pure, CI-testable parser that emits the existing `ParseResult` contract, and add the additive `kind?: 'expense' | 'credit'` field to `RawTransaction` so estornos/credits (D-04) can be represented end-to-end. This is the core-value code of Phase 13: everything downstream of `ParseResult` is reused unchanged.

Purpose: Per the spike (13-RESEARCH.md), `getText()` — NOT `getTable()` — is the workhorse on real Santander PDFs. The parser MUST split into `extractPdfText(buffer)` (the only async/IO part) + `parseSantanderText(text, venc)` (pure, unit-testable against a committed synthetic text fixture, since real PDFs stay gitignored). This split is what makes PDF-02/PDF-04 testable in CI without shipping personal financial data.

Output: `pdf-parse@^2.4.5` + `unpdf@^1.6.2` installed and provenance-verified; `src/lib/parsers/pdf.ts`; `kind?` on `RawTransaction`; `tests/fixtures/santander-sample.txt` (synthetic); `pdf.test.ts`; an extended `dedupe.test.ts`.
</objective>
