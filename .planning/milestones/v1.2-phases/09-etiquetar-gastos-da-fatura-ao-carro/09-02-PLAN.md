<objective>

novo/editar lançamento dialog (with "Nenhum" to clear), a per-row "Vincular a carro" action in
the Extrato table that links/unlinks a single transaction, and a "Vincular a carro" mode in the

extrato), free of category, non-destructively.

extrato RSC wiring (carros list + carro_id on rows).
</objective>
