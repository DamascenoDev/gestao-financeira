## How it went (the smoke was a debugging session)

The Phase 14 storage path worked immediately; the headline AI suggestion took a long
diagnosis because every failure was **external/provider**, not the code:

1. **flash-lite 503 "high demand"** (shipped default) — transient free-tier congestion;
   CLSAI-06 degraded to empty Map (no crash), so no suggestion that upload.
2. Switched to **`gemini-3.5-flash`** → it's a **thinking model**: the 1-token
   `testConnection` ping stalled ~50s then failed; risky near the 60s function limit.
3. Switched to **`gemini-2.0-flash`** → **429 RESOURCE_EXHAUSTED, free_tier limit:0** —
   it is **paid-tier-only** for this key. (AI Studio rate-limit page confirmed free quota
   exists only for `gemini-2.5-flash-lite` and `gemini-3.5-flash`.)
4. **Reverted to `gemini-2.5-flash-lite`** (free, non-thinking) → on a non-congested
   retry the classify returned a real suggestion. ✓

Two other dead-ends along the way were data/observability, not bugs in the AI path:
the Vercel "External APIs" panel is incomplete (don't trust it for "no outgoing call");
and re-uploading the *same file* short-circuits on `content_hash` (see findings).
